Privacy & Data Use

Lan Read is local-first. Your library stays on-device unless you turn on optional CloudKit sync or use secure key exchange to fetch your own AI credentials. No mandatory accounts, minimal telemetry focused on reliability.

Key Points English

  • Local-first storage; books and highlights remain on your devices by default.
  • CloudKit sync is optional and uses your Apple ID; you control when it is enabled.
  • AI key exchange happens via your own FastAPI endpoint; credentials are not stored in the app bundle.
  • No required sign-up; AI requests avoid attaching account identifiers where possible.
  • Telemetry is minimal and reliability-focused (crash diagnostics and request timing only).

要点 中文

  • 默认本地优先，书籍与标注仅保留在你的设备上。
  • CloudKit 同步完全可选，使用你的 Apple ID，开启与否由你决定。
  • AI 密钥通过自建 FastAPI 下发，凭证不会写入 App 本地包内。
  • 无需注册账号，AI 请求尽量不携带身份标识。
  • 仅保留与稳定性相关的最小遥测（崩溃与请求耗时）。
